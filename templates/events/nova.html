Online Fashion Design and Photoshoot Competition

    Rules
    • There is no registration fee for this event.
    • Team should consist of a model , a designer and a photographer. Model and Designer can be the same.
    • Minimum age limit is 18.
    • A maximum of 5 Photos should be posted in Facebook or Instagram with a caption explaining about the theme which you chose for making the design. You should Tag @alcheringaiitg and @voguenation_alcheriitg for Instagram and(Alcheringa, IIT Guwahati, Vogue Nation,Alcheringa)pages for facebook.
    • Link of your post should be submitted at www.alcheringa.in/registrations/
    • The quality of the photo(s) should be clear and editing of photos is allowed.
    • The decision of the organisers will be the final one.
    • Props are allowed but will not fetch any extra points or be a part of the judging criteria.
    • The decision taken by the organisers is final and binding for the performers.
    • Vulgarity in any form will not be allowed in the competition.
    • Organisers reserve the right to disqualify teams who do not adhere to the rules.
