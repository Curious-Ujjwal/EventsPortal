    Rules
    • There is no registration fee for this event.
    • The theme as well as the genre of the short movie are not imposed.
    • The short movie should not last for more than 12 minutes (credits included).
    • Suitable for all audiences: No obscenity. No Nudity. No Excessive violence. SHORT MOVIE deemed obscene or defamatory will be disqualified.
    • Short movies have to be shot in either Hindi or English.
    • You have to submit the Google Drive Link of the video at www.alcheringa.in/registrations/ with provided access.
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding in all cases.
    • Organizers have full authority to disqualify the participation in case of any discrepancy.
    Judging Criteria
    1. Story Telling
    2. Creativity and Originality
    3. Screenplay and Actor's performance.
    4. Technical Execution: Camera / Sound / Light / Editing
    5. Overall Impression
